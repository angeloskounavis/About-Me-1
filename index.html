About Me

Hey There!

My name's Ian. I'm currently attending Code Fellows Tech Academy, and before this I was in sales at a concrete company (super exciting stuff, I know). Before working at the concrete company I had all kinds of jobs; I was a fraud investigator at Amazon, and English teacher in China, a waiter, a cook and even a wildland firefighter at one point.

I went to school at the University of Oregon, where I got a bachelor's degree in Chinese, with a minor in French.

I'm looking forward to building my skill set during this boot camp, so that I can get a job that will give me a better life, and hopefully I'll be able to use those skills to do some good in this world!
